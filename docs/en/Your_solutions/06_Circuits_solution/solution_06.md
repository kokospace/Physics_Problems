# Power and Energy Calculation

## Problem

A voltage of 50 V is applied across a 100 Ω resistor.

Find:

1. The power dissipated by the resistor
2. The energy consumed in 5 minutes

## Given Data

- Voltage, V = 50 V
- Resistance, R = 100 Ω
- Time, t = 5 minutes = 300 seconds

## Formula Used

Power dissipated by a resistor:

P = V² / R

Energy consumed:

E = P × t

## Solution

First, calculate the power:

P = 50² / 100

P = 2500 / 100

P = 25 W

Now calculate the energy consumed in 5 minutes:

E = 25 × 300

E = 7500 J

E = 7.5 kJ

## Final Answer

The power dissipated by the resistor is:

25 W

The energy consumed in 5 minutes is:

7500 J, or 7.5 kJ
