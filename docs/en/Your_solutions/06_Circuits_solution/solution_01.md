# Problem 1: Series and Parallel Circuit

Given:
R1 = 15 Ω  
R2 = 30 Ω  
R3 = 50 Ω  
V = 12 V  

---

## 1. Series Connection

Equivalent resistance:
Req = R1 + R2 + R3  
Req = 15 + 30 + 50 = 95 Ω  

Current from battery:
I = V / Req  
I = 12 / 95 ≈ 0.126 A  

---

## 2. Parallel Connection

Equivalent resistance:
1/Req = 1/R1 + 1/R2 + 1/R3  
1/Req = 1/15 + 1/30 + 1/50  

Common denominator = 150:
1/Req = (10 + 5 + 3) / 150  
1/Req = 18 / 150  

Req = 150 / 18 ≈ 8.33 Ω  

Current from battery:
I = V / Req  
I = 12 / 8.33 ≈ 1.44 A  

---

## Final Answers:

Series:
Req = 95 Ω  
I ≈ 0.126 A  

Parallel:
Req ≈ 8.33 Ω  
I ≈ 1.44 A
