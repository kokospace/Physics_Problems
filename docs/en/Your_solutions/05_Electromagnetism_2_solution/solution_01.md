# Problem 1: Gauss's Law

## Given:
Point charge: Q = +2 C  
Permittivity of free space: ε₀ = 8.85 × 10⁻¹²  
Spherical surface radius: 1 m 

---

## Formula:
Φ = Q / ε₀

---

## Calculation:
Φ = 2 / (8.85 × 10⁻¹²)  
Φ ≈ 2.26 × 10¹¹ N·m²/C

---

## Final Answer:
Φ ≈ 2.26 × 10¹¹ N·m²/C

---

## Note:
The radius of the sphere does NOT affect the result.  
Gauss's Law depends only on the enclosed charge.
