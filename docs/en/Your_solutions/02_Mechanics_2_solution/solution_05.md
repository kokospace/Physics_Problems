## 🧮 Inelastic Collision

### 📌 Formula (Conservation of Momentum)

m₁v₁ + m₂v₂ = (m₁ + m₂)v_f

---

### 📊 Given:

- m₁ = 70 kg (runner)
- v₁ = 3 m/s
- m₂ = 140 kg (cart)
- v₂ = 0 m/s

---

### 🔹 Solution:

70 × 3 + 140 × 0 = (70 + 140) v_f

210 = 210 v_f

v_f = 1 m/s

---

### ✅ Final Velocity:

v_f = 1 m/s

---

### ⚠️ Kinetic Energy Check:

Initial KE:
KE_i = ½ × 70 × 3² = 315 J

Final KE:
KE_f = ½ × 210 × 1² = 105 J

---

### ❗ Conclusion:

- Momentum is conserved ✅  
- Kinetic energy is NOT conserved ❌  
- This is a perfectly inelastic collision
