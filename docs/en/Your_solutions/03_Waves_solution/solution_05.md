## Echo Problem

A person shouts towards a cliff and hears the echo after 1 second.  
The speed of sound in air is 343 m/s.

---

###  Formula

d = (v × t) / 2

---

###  Calculation

d = (343 × 1) / 2  
d = 171.5 m

---

###  Final Answer

**The cliff is 171.5 meters away.**
